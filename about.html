About KC Pros Electric

KC Pros Electric was formed in October 2016. It was an idea of Steve Turner and Stephon Blake(owners) to build a electric company that would provide excellent customer service, build strong relationships with our customers and create some of the best electrical installations around.


Steve and Stephon have worked in the Kansas City area for over 30 years and understand the market very well. While we are starting our business in KC area our cole is to eventually end up at the Lake of the Ozarks with a second location and take our expertise to customers there.
